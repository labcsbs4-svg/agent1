Our Products

At Creative Solutions Inc., we offer three core products designed to help businesses operate more efficiently, make better decisions, and stay connected with their customers. Each product is built with modern technology, intuitive design, and scalability in mind—whether you're a small startup or a growing enterprise.

1. InnovateHub — Project Management Made Simple

Price: $50 per user per month

InnovateHub is our all-in-one project management platform created to help teams plan, collaborate, and execute their work with clarity. It brings together task tracking, team communication, file sharing, and workflow automation into one clean, easy-to-use interface.

Key features include:

Visual task boards (Kanban, calendar, timelines)

Real-time team chat and project discussions

Automatic progress updates and smart reminders

Integration with tools like Slack, Drive, and GitHub

Detailed analytics to monitor time, workloads, and deadlines

Whether your team is working remotely or in-office, InnovateHub keeps everyone aligned and moving forward.

2. DataSphere — Turn Data Into Decisions

DataSphere is our advanced analytics and business intelligence platform built for organizations that want to make smarter, data-driven decisions. It helps simplify complex datasets and transforms them into clear dashboards, reports, and predictive insights.

Highlights:

Interactive dashboards with customizable visualizations

AI-powered trend detection and forecasting

Secure data connectors (SQL, cloud storage, APIs, spreadsheets)

Automated report generation and email scheduling

Role-based access control for secure collaboration

DataSphere is ideal for teams looking to understand performance, identify trends, or reveal hidden opportunities.

3. ConnectFlow — Smart, Modern CRM

ConnectFlow is our customer relationship management system designed to help businesses improve their sales processes, strengthen customer relationships, and grow revenue. It puts customer insights and communication tools in one organized space.

What it offers:

Centralized customer and lead profiles

Email, call, and meeting tracking

Sales pipeline visualization

Automated follow-ups and workflow triggers

Reporting tools for forecasting and performance tracking

From startups to established teams, ConnectFlow makes building strong customer relationships simpler and more effective.
